Support Center

Help & Guidance

ClearLedger is designed to keep business records organized without forcing users through technical steps. This page gives administrators and business users a simple place to understand what each area does, where to go next, and how to move through daily work with confidence.

Getting Started

  • Start in the Dashboard to see the current business overview.
  • Use Summary to review totals, activity, and overall business movement.
  • Keep entries current so reports, tax prep, and reviews stay clean and reliable.

Daily Recordkeeping

  • Manage billing work inside Invoices.
  • Use Team Members and Team Member Payouts to manage labor records and payouts.
  • Track field costs with Gas, Materials, and Mileage.

Communication & Review

  • Use Messenger for business-related communication inside the app.
  • Use Reports when you need organized output for review or printing.
  • Visit IRS Tips for general reminders and tax-support guidance.

Where to Find Things

Administrator Area
Use this area to manage businesses, review the system at a high level, and enter a selected business workspace.

Business Workspace
This is the dedicated operating area for one selected business. Use the workspace modules to move through overview, records, communication, and reports.

Settings & Support
Use the existing profile, user, email, and help areas to keep setup organized and records accurate.

Recommended Workflow

  • Review the Dashboard first.
  • Update income records, team members, and payouts as activity happens.
  • Record gas, materials, and mileage regularly instead of waiting until the end of the month.
  • Use Summary and Reports to check totals before sharing, printing, or preparing for bookkeeping review.
  • Use Messenger when communication needs to stay connected to the business workflow.

General Support Guidance

  • Confirm the correct active business before entering or editing records.
  • Review key information before printing reports or sharing data with others.
  • Use the Help page as a quick reference whenever you are unsure where a feature lives.
  • Use IRS Tips for general tax guidance, but keep business records updated inside the normal workflow pages.
