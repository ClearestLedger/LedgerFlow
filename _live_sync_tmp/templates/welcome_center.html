Start Here
  1. Review your main dashboard and confirm the business name, owner details, and current workspace status.
  2. Open Billing to confirm the subscription tier, billing status, and method on file.
  3. Use Income Records to begin saving business income in a tax-preparation-friendly format.
  4. Open Team Members if you need to add, update, or organize staff and payout information.
  5. Use Calendar and reports as your operational planning layer once the basics are in place.
