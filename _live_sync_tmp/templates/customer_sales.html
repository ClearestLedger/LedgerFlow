How This Flow Works

Estimate → Invoice
  1. Create the estimate first when the customer still needs to review the scope or approve pricing.
  2. Send or copy the hosted estimate link so the customer can review it online.
  3. Convert approved work into the final invoice when you are ready to collect payment.
  4. Use customer invoices and reminders for collections, and keep bookkeeping-only income records separate.
