How Estimate Flow Works

Sales Workflow
  1. Create the estimate with line items and send it to the customer.
  2. The customer opens a hosted page and can approve or decline it.
  3. Once approved, convert it into a real invoice in one click.
  4. Send the final invoice from the invoice center when work is ready to bill.
This keeps the estimate phase separate from income tracking, so your reports and billing stay accurate.
